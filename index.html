Our Heritage

Why Choose Artha Interior Designers?

At Artha Interior Designers, our carpentry team brings generations of experience, with woodworking being a proud family tradition passed down over the years. This heritage, combined with modern techniques, allows us to deliver exceptional craftsmanship with precision and attention to detail.

Unlike factory-manufactured modular installations, we execute all carpentry work directly at your site. This ensures complete transparency throughout the process, allows for customization based on your space, and gives our clients full visibility into the quality of materials and workmanship.

We believe in the efficient utilization of every material, minimizing wastage without compromising on quality. Our skilled craftsmen work with accuracy, neatness, and professionalism, ensuring a clean and well-organized work environment.

From the initial 3D design and rendering to the final installation, every stage is carried out with meticulous planning and expert execution. Our commitment is to deliver premium finishes, durable workmanship, and interiors that exceed our clients' expectations.

At Artha Interior Designers, we don't just build interiors—we craft timeless spaces that combine beauty, functionality, and lasting value.

How We Work

Four stages, one clear timeline

Every project moves through the same sequence, so you always know what stage you're at and what comes next.

01

Consultation & Brief

We walk your space, understand budget and lifestyle, and translate both into a design brief before a single sketch is made.

02

Design & 3D Walkthrough

Layouts, material palettes and a full 3D walkthrough are shared for your sign-off — nothing moves to site until you approve it.

03

Material & Vendor Selection

We shortlist and finalize materials, fittings and vendors against the approved design, so quality is locked in before execution starts.

04

Execution & Handover

Site work is managed against a fixed schedule with regular site visits, ending in a final walkthrough before keys are handed back.
