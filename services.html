How We Work

Four stages, one clear timeline

Every project moves through the same sequence, so you always know what stage you're at and what comes next.

01

Consultation & Brief

We walk your space, understand budget and lifestyle, and translate both into a design brief before a single sketch is made.

02

Design & 3D Walkthrough

Layouts, material palettes and a full 3D walkthrough are shared for your sign-off — nothing moves to site until you approve it.

03

Material & Vendor Selection

We shortlist and finalize materials, fittings and vendors against the approved design, so quality is locked in before execution starts.

04

Execution & Handover

Site work is managed against a fixed schedule with regular site visits, ending in a final walkthrough before keys are handed back.
